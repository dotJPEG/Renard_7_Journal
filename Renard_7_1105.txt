What are strings?
Strings are a data type that is consited of a 'string' of characters  
eg. "asdf1234!@#$"

What are they good for?
They are good for recording data in a way that humans can read, you cant really
store a sentance in an integer.

What are some string methods?
A string method is a method that uses a string or searches a string.

How do we identify string methods? 
String methods use Strings or search strings to accomplish what they are used
for using and locating strings.
